---
title: Launcher
description: Use Portainer inventory as a lightweight application catalog.
---

Launcher shows discovered containers and manually configured services in one searchable view. Unsupported applications receive a compact card with their name, image, state, and launch action instead of a pretend native dashboard.

## Customize an entry

You can rename an entry, replace its inferred URL, or hide it. These overrides are stored separately from discovered data and survive future Portainer synchronization.

## Missing containers

When a container disappears, Dashbored marks it unavailable before pruning old uncustomized records. Customized entries are retained so a redeployment does not silently discard your organization.
