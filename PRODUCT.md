# Dashbored

## Product

Dashbored is a self-hosted control plane that brings service health, summaries, history, safe actions, and launch links into one dashboard.

## Audience

The primary user is the owner or operator of a small self-hosted environment. The interface assumes technical familiarity but should make routine operations faster than opening each upstream application.

## Purpose

Provide one precise, calm view of infrastructure, network, monitoring, downloads, automation, and media services on a trusted private network.

## Personality

Precise, minimal, fast, trustworthy, and operational. The product should feel like a professional deployment console, not a decorative smart-home dashboard.

## Reference

The visual reference is the Vercel dashboard and the published Geist design system: neutral surfaces, compact typography, hairline borders, minimal elevation, dense navigation, and restrained semantic color.

## Anti-references

- Warm paper-like backgrounds
- Decorative grid or dot patterns behind the application
- Tiny, widely tracked uppercase labels used everywhere
- Floating card grids with heavy shadows or hover movement
- Excessive animation and ornamental gradients

## Accessibility

Meet WCAG AA contrast, retain visible keyboard focus, provide 44px touch targets where needed on mobile, and honor reduced-motion preferences.
